# Kyle & Jill's St. Moritz Wedding Adventure

**The world's first (and only) wedding-themed snowboarding game.**

Because nothing says "I do" like shredding down the Swiss Alps at 60mph while collecting rosé and dodging trees.

---

## The Plot

Kyle and Jill are getting married in St. Moritz. But instead of arriving to their wedding events like normal human beings (car, taxi, walking), they decided to **snowboard across the entire town** while doing backflips, collecting cryptocurrency, and drinking rosé.

As one does.

## Choose Your Fighter

- **Jill** — The Glamorous Skier. Elegant on every slope. Will absolutely judge your form.
- **Kyle** — The Crypto Shredder. To the moon! Still checking his portfolio mid-run.
- **Banksy** — The Wedding Pup. The one and only. Honestly the best option.

## The Wedding Events

Shred your way through all five real wedding events:

1. **Welcome Aperitif** — Badrutt's Palace (yes, THE Badrutt's Palace, with the turrets and everything)
2. **Rehearsal Dinner** — Grand Hotel Kronenhof, Pontresina
3. **Après Ski** — White Marmot, Corviglia
4. **THE WEDDING** — Paradiso, St. Moritz (the game literally stops so they can kiss, it's adorable)
5. **After Party** — Embassy Ballroom (disco lights included)

## Features That Absolutely No One Asked For

- A full **procedural EDM soundtrack** inspired by "We Found Love" by Calvin Harris & Rihanna, because of course
- **Backflip combos** with multipliers, because this is a serious extreme sports game
- **A coin magnet power-up** that activates after collecting 10 $SOL, which is somehow the most realistic crypto mechanic ever built
- **A wedding kiss cutscene** where the game pauses, confetti explodes, and Kyle & Jill smooch while a heart beats above them
- **High scores saved locally** so you can compete with your wedding guests over who's the best virtual snowboarder
- The St. Moritz sun logo **actually spins** on the title screen. You're welcome.

## How to Play

- **Tap / Click / Spacebar** — Jump
- **Tap in air** — Do tricks (backflips, grabs, spins)
- **Chain tricks** — Combo multiplier goes brrr
- **Don't hit trees** — Just kidding, there's no collision damage. It's a wedding game, not Dark Souls.

## Tech Stack

- One single `index.html` file
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Canvas 2D
- Web Audio API (the entire soundtrack is generated in real-time, no audio files)
- Zero dependencies
- Zero build tools
- Approximately zero regard for software engineering best practices
